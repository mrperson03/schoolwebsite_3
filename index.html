Campus Life

School Library

Was refurbished in 2022

Here the students can take out books and read, as well as do some fun activities with the school librarian. We go here once or twice a week and keep returning and exchanging books for new ones.



School Gym

Was built over 50 years ago

We go here to get some physical activity and we get to play games, like dodgeball, soccer, basketball, and others. We also go here when we have an assembly, and the whole school comes to the gym to watch.

School Achievments

Track and field competition
Got 4th place in 200m run

In track and field, I got 4th place representing our school in the 200m run. I also participated in the 60m relay that had 4 people as a team. Our team did not do good in this event but that was okay. I really liked this event and I am very happy for this achievment.



Science fair
Got 1st place in science fair

Everybody in the school had to make a poster board about a science experiment showing how it works and their procedure/results. I got 1st place in this event



Heritage fair
Did well in heritage fair.

This was an class activity where you had to make a poster board highlighting a major part in canadian history. We had to write information and include pictures. We were not given an individual ranking for this project.

What's new

  • New school parking lot

    Last year, our parking lot was small and it was very crowded with cars and people because there wasn't enough space in the parking lot. So over the summer the school rebuilt the parking lot and added mor car spots and a lot more space. They also added traffic lights to control all the cars coming in the morningtime. This made it a safer place for kids to come to school.



  • New Principal

    At the start of the year, we got a new principal. Our old principal moved to a different school, and our new principal came from a different school. Since school has just started, I haven't seen her very much, but she seems nice.
